## Mini-Presentation Practice: deliver a short data story presentation 
Prepare to present a data story to us for up to 5 minutes
There are broadly three approaches to this:
-  Pick an existing visual data story and prepare a spoken presentation of it
-  Make your own data story (from my data or any source) and prepare a spoken presentation of it
-  Make your own data story and prepare a free-standing presentation of it (this presentation will be mostly silent)

In all cases, sketches, rough ideas and notes are all acceptable: it is the purpose and focus of the data story which we need to receive
The presentation is the key here, not the data you choose to use or even completedness

You have 30 minutes to prepare then up to five minutes to present

**A cautious suggestion:** if permitted within your organization, you could use this time to see (given some data and some prompting) what a (free) machine-based tool can generate

Everyone will receive constructive feedback
